How We Build SaaS

Focus on delivering value to customers from day one, while we provide you with all the components your SaaS application needs out of the box.

The 8gears SaaS Factory and its experts specialized on transforming existing or legacy and on-prem software, data or APIs into Cloud Native SaaS solution at manageable costs. Our focus and rich set of standardized and modular SaaS components enables us to transform SaaS solutions 5x faster and at a lower cost.

We primarily focus on expanding our own portfolio, of SaaS solutions with e.g. container-registry.com, z-rechnung(exit), but occasionally we also help others do the same.

SaaS Factory Components

Subscription Management

End-to-end subscription management with recurring billing, invoicing, payments, accounting and financial transactions.

Authentication and Authorization

Simple and secure user registration, login and access control.

Rollout and Operation

Deployment of the entire cloud infrastructure and migration of the application for scalable operation in the cloud.

Multi-tenancy

SaaS Integration Platform

Your SaaS should not be a data silo. Your users want an automatic and smooth data exchange with other SaaS solutions.
